White Cake

Ingredients: Sugar, bleached enriched wheat flour (flour, malted barley flour, niacin, reduced iron, thiamine mononitrate, riboflavin, folic acid), water, soybean oil, eggs, egg whites, high fructose corn syrup. Contains 2% or less of each of the following: milk protein concentrate, salt, cornstarch, wheat starch, whey, sodium acid pyrophosphate, nonfat milk, baking soda, natural and artificial flavor, soy lecithin, mono- and diglycerides, cellulose gum, gum arabic, isolated soy protein, lactose, palm oil, monocalcium phosphate, xanthan gum, guar gum, polyglycerol esters of fatty acids, propylene glycol mono- and diesters of fats and fatty acids, yellow corn flour, datem, sodium aluminum phosphate, aluminum sulfate.

Allergy Information: Contains eggs, milk, soy, wheat. Manufactured in a shared facility with: walnuts, coconut.

Contains 2% or less of each of the following: natural and artificial flavor, food starch-modified, salt, baking soda, sodium acid pyrophosphate, soy lecithin, calcium sulfate, mono- and diglycerides, cellulose gum, xanthan gum, polyglycerol esters of fatty acids, monocalcium phosphate, propylene glycol mono- and diesters of fats and fatty acids, datem, sodium aluminum phosphate, aluminum sulfate, sodium benzoate (preservative).

Allergy Information: Contains eggs, milk, soy, wheat. Manufactured in a shared facility with: walnuts, coconut.
